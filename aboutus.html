Who We Are

When I moved to Salt Lake City in 2017, I fell immediately in love with the staggering size and beauty of the Wasatch Mountains. Towering over the Salt Lake Valley, the recreation possibilities are endless, including a staggering 9 world class ski resorts within less than an hours driving distance.

Wasatch Escapes was created to share the love of these mountains and ski resorts with the rest of the world. To connect others to the beauty and sense of wonder that I experienced the first time I laid eyes on these mountains, and continue to experience to this day.

Explore our site, linking you to current conditions and information from several of the resorts in the Wasatch. Make the all important decision of which resort to patronize based on the amount and quality of snow that fell overnight.

At Wasatch Escapes we are ski & snowboard enthusiasts just like you. We live to get first chair on a powder day in Little Cottonwood canyon, and experience the epicness that comes from it. We love Utah, and though every day in the Wasatch is a great day, we want to help make it specially awesome for you!
